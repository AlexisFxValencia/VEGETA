VEGETA - Visualizer & Exporter for GEometries of Tridimentional Aspect

Help

Basics :

Principle : Copy-paste your text input on the left, click on "Plot" and wait for the generation of your 3D view

MORET 5 is selected by default but you can change the neutronic code used on the left

The name of the volume and its material is displayed on top of the viewer when the mouse hovers on it

Get a volume transparent :

    You can get a mesh transparent by :
  • right clicking on it
  • click on the checkbox on the left corresponding to the name of the volume

Generate a 3D export :

Mainly use .ply (includes colors)
If you want multiple 3D pieces, you have to adapt the view to the export you want (get every unecessary volume transparent).
